  <h1>Welcome to HL7 Austria Education</h1>
  
  <p>This site provides links and information to help you educate yourself on HL7 standards in Austria. Whether you're a healthcare professional, developer, or student, you'll find resources for learning about various health information standards.</p>

  <h2>Available Standards</h2>
  
  <div class="standards-overview">
    <h3><a href="{{ '/dicom.html' | relative_url }}">DICOM</a></h3>
    <p>Digital Imaging and Communications in Medicine - the standard for medical imaging information.</p>

    <h3><a href="{{ '/cda.html' | relative_url }}">CDA</a></h3>
    <p>Clinical Document Architecture - an XML-based markup standard for clinical document exchange.</p>

    <h3><a href="{{ '/snomed-ct.html' | relative_url }}">SNOMED CT</a></h3>
    <p>Systematized Nomenclature of Medicine - Clinical Terms - a comprehensive clinical terminology.</p>

    <h3><a href="{{ '/fhir.html' | relative_url }}">FHIR</a></h3>
    <p>Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources - the next generation standard for healthcare data exchange.</p>
  </div>

  <h2>About HL7 Austria</h2>
  <p>HL7 Austria is the Austrian affiliate of Health Level Seven International, dedicated to promoting and advancing healthcare information standards in Austria.</p>
